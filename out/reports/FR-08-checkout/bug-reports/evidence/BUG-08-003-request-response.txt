BUG-08-003 — POST /api/apply-coupon enforces no authentication at all (C4/C5 bypass)
Captured 2026-07-04, Continuation FR-08 Full, ref TC-08-EP-008/ER-08-EP-008 and TC-08-DT-002/ER-08-DT-002

=== Scenario A: spoofed identity, no token (TC-08-EP-008) ===

--- Request ---
POST /api/apply-coupon HTTP/1.1
Host: localhost:3000
Content-Type: application/json
(no Authorization header)

{"code":"VIP100","total_amount":400000,"user_id":1}

--- Response ---
200 OK
{"success":true,"coupon_id":3,"discount_amount":100000,"final_amount":300000,"message":"Áp dụng thành công! Giảm 100,000 ₫"}

Observation: user_id 1 is the admin account's id. The requester presented no JWT of any kind,
yet received a successful discount computed as if authenticated as user 1.

=== Scenario B: confirmed-exhausted usage, no token, user_id omitted (TC-08-DT-002) ===

--- Setup: record usage as the real, authenticated owner ---
POST /api/coupon-usage HTTP/1.1
Authorization: Bearer <test@eshop.com JWT>
{"coupon_id":1}
--- Response ---
200 OK  {"message":"Usage recorded"}

--- Sanity check: authenticated re-attempt confirms exhaustion ---
POST /api/apply-coupon HTTP/1.1
Authorization: Bearer <test@eshop.com JWT>
{"code":"SAVE10","total_amount":500000,"user_id":2}
--- Response ---
400 Bad Request
{"error":"Bạn đã sử dụng mã này 1 lần (đã đạt giới hạn)"}

--- Request: same coupon, no token, user_id omitted entirely ---
POST /api/apply-coupon HTTP/1.1
Host: localhost:3000
Content-Type: application/json
(no Authorization header)

{"code":"SAVE10","total_amount":500000}

--- Response ---
200 OK
{"success":true,"coupon_id":1,"discount_amount":-4500000,"final_amount":5000000,"message":"Áp dụng thành công! Giảm 10%"}

Observation: moments after an authenticated request confirmed this coupon/user combination was
exhausted, the identical coupon succeeded unconditionally the instant the `user_id` field was
simply omitted from the body and no token was presented — both README FR-09 C4 (must have a
valid JWT) and C5 (uses-per-user cap) were bypassed by omitting one JSON field.
